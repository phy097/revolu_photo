Spring Revolution

The Spring Revolution in Myanmar, sparked by the military coup in February 2021, saw widespread protests and civil disobedience movements across the country. Despite facing brutal crackdowns by the military junta, the people of Myanmar demonstrated remarkable resilience and determination in their fight for democracy and the restoration of civilian rule.

Rising Against Repression
The Spring Revolution in Myanmar

The impact of the Spring Revolution in Myanmar has been profound, signaling a widespread rejection of military rule and a fervent demand for democracy and human rights. Despite facing violent repression, the courageous actions of protesters have brought international attention to Myanmar's struggle for freedom and justice. The ongoing resistance has inspired hope for a future where the will of the people prevails over authoritarianism, fostering a sense of unity and resilience among Myanmar's diverse population.

About Me

As a street photographer hailing from Yangon, Myanmar, I am deeply passionate about capturing the raw beauty and essence of urban life. With a keen eye for detail and a love for storytelling, I aim to document the diverse tapestry of cultures, traditions, and everyday moments that define my city. Through my lens, I strive to evoke emotions and share the unique narratives of life on the vibrant streets of Yangon.
